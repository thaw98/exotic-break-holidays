Welcome to Exotic Breaks!

Treat yourself to the ultimate break by escaping to one of our exotic holiday destinations. Imagine lying on a white sandy beach with coconut palms swaying in the breeze, cocktails at hand and the smell of spice in the air.

Exotic destinations such as the Caribbean are ever more accessible and just waiting to be discovered by you.

Exotic Breaks can also take you off the beaten track and lead you to discover all that the Far East and Asia have to offer. Combine adventure with relaxation and head into the Indian Ocean and the islands of the Maldives.

Over the summer months, why not try a little adventure and fly to Kenya to experience a breathtaking safan, During July and August the water level is lower and wild animals flock to water holes making the sighting of incerdible beasts such as lions, elephants and zebra even more likely for onlookers.

Whatever exotic break you are craving, the perfect solution is out there for you!
